Let's talk about your project

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do I own my website when it's done?
Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the code, and every file. If you ever want to move your site somewhere else, you can. There's no lock-in with Fluxor.
Will my website show up on Google?
We submit your site to Google Search Console on launch day and set up everything Google needs — sitemap, meta tags, and analytics. Most new sites appear in Google within 4–8 weeks. Local SEO results typically follow within 2–3 months.
I'm not tech-savvy — is this going to be confusing?
Not at all. You fill out a simple form, review a preview link, and we handle everything technical. We'll walk you through everything at handoff and you'll get a plain-English guide explaining how your site works.
What if I want to make changes after launch?
Every plan already includes 1 content refresh per month at no extra charge. Bigger changes, like adding new pages or a redesign, are quoted separately at a flat rate — no hourly billing surprises.
How much does it cost to keep my site running?
Your monthly plan covers everything: domain renewal, Cloudflare security and hosting fees, DNS maintenance, and uptime monitoring. There's no separate hosting bill to worry about — it's all bundled into your one monthly payment.
Can you build a website for a construction or trade business?
Absolutely — it's one of our most common requests. We build sites for contractors, roofers, landscapers, electricians, and other trades all the time. We know how to present your work, highlight your service area, and make your phone number impossible to miss.
Do you work with businesses outside of Vancouver?
Yes — we work with businesses all across Canada. The entire process happens online, so location is never a barrier. We've helped clients from Victoria to Toronto get online.
